More visibility and response.

Your advertisement deserves the top spot! Enjoy more views and responses than ordinary ads, as Top Ads replicate your listing, securing you a prime position for selling. It's a clear choice: the more eyes on your ad, the greater your chances of a successful sale!

This feature highlights a maximum of five Top Ads at any given time within the dedicated Top Ad section, positioned prominently at the top of search results. This ensures optimal visibility for your listings, increasing their exposure and potential for engagement.
In instances where there are more than five Top Ads within the same category, a rotation mechanism is implemented. This ensures that each Top Ad receives equal exposure by rotating them each time the page is loaded, allowing fair visibility for all sellers.
Top Ads duplicate your listing for 30 days, ensuring prime visibility and increased responses. This feature maximizes your selling opportunities by consistently placing your listing in a prominent position, making it easily accessible to a broader audience.
