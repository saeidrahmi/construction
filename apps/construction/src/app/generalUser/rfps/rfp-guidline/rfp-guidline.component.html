Construction Request for Proposal (RFP)

Introduction and Project Overview

  • Provide a brief introduction to your organization.
  • Outline the purpose and goals of the construction project.
  • Clearly state the timeline and key milestones.

Project Scope

  • Define the scope of work in detail.
  • Specify the types of construction services required.
  • Outline any specific materials, methods, or technologies to be used.

Qualification Requirements

  • Clearly state the qualifications required for contractors.
  • Include information on licensing, bonding, insurance, and relevant certifications.
  • Specify the minimum experience and track record needed.

Submission Instructions

  • Clearly outline the submission process and deadline.
  • Include details on the format and method of submission.
  • Specify any required documentation or forms.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Clearly define the criteria by which proposals will be evaluated.
  • Include factors such as cost, experience, qualifications, and proposed timeline.
  • Provide weightings for each criterion if applicable.

Budget Information

  • Provide a budget range or estimate for the project.
  • Clearly outline any budgetary constraints.
  • Indicate whether alternative proposals will be considered.

Site Visit Information

  • Specify whether a site visit is mandatory.
  • Provide details on the date, time, and location of the site visit.
  • Include contact information for coordinating site visits.

Timeline and Milestones

  • Clearly outline the project timeline, including key milestones.
  • Specify the start and end dates of the project.
  • Include any critical dates or deadlines.

Contract Terms and Conditions

  • Provide information on contract terms and conditions.
  • Outline payment terms, deliverables, and performance guarantees.
  • Include any legal or regulatory requirements.

Proposal Format

  • Specify the required format for proposals.
  • Include any specific sections or information that must be included.
  • Provide guidance on how to structure the proposal.

Communication and Questions

  • Specify the point of contact for communication and questions.
  • Provide contact information for inquiries.
  • Establish a timeline for responding to questions and issuing clarifications.

Legal and Compliance Requirements

  • Outline any legal or compliance requirements contractors must adhere to.
  • Specify any permits or approvals needed for the project.

Insurance and Liability

  • Clearly outline insurance requirements for contractors.
  • Specify liability limits and coverage types.

Confidentiality and Proprietary Information

  • Clearly state any confidentiality requirements.
  • Specify how proprietary information will be handled.

Review and Selection Process

  • Provide an overview of the review and selection process.
  • Specify the criteria and timeline for the selection decision.
  • Outline any negotiation or clarification processes.

Appendix and Attachments

  • Include any necessary appendices or attachments.
  • Provide relevant drawings, specifications, or additional project documentation.

Terms of Agreement

  • Clearly state that the issuance of an RFP does not commit your organization to accept any proposal.
  • Specify that your organization reserves the right to reject any or all proposals.

Instructions for Proposal Modifications

  • Outline the process for submitting modifications to proposals.
  • Provide a deadline for any revisions.

Post-Award Information

  • Briefly outline what will happen after the proposal is awarded.
  • Include information on contract signing and project commencement.
